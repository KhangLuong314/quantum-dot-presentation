Theory

Time-Independent Schr√∂dinger Equation

\( \hat{H} \psi(x) = E \psi(x) \)

\( \frac{d^2 \psi(x)}{dx^2} = \frac{-2m}{\hbar^2} [E - V(x)] \psi(x) \)

Theory

Since \( V(x) = 0 \quad \text{for} \quad 0 < x < a \)

Let \( k = \sqrt{ \frac{2m \left[ E - V(x) \right]}{\hbar^2} } \)

\( \psi_n(x) = \sqrt{\frac{2}{a}} \sin\left( \frac{n\pi x}{a} \right)\)

for n = 1,2,3,...

\( E_n = \frac{\hbar^2 \pi^2 n^2}{2 m a^2} \)

If \( a = r_0 \Rightarrow E_n = \frac{\hbar^2 \pi^2 n^2}{2 m r_0^2} \)

Theory

\(E_g\) is the difference in energy between \(E_e \; and \; E_h\)

\(E_{emission} = E_g + E_e + E_h\)

Theory

\(E_{emission} = E_g + \frac{\hbar^2\pi^2n^2}{2a^2}(\frac{1}{m_e} + \frac{1}{m_h})\)

Theory

\(E_{emission} = E_g + \frac{\hbar^2\pi^2}{2a^2} \left( \frac{1}{m_e} + \frac{1}{m_h} \right)\)

Procedure
